Our Story

Born from a
Love of Craft

Bella Vogue was founded in 2010 by creative director Isabella Fontaine — a woman who believed that fashion should feel as extraordinary as it looks. From a small atelier in Milan, the brand has grown into a global symbol of understated luxury.

Every Bella Vogue garment begins with the finest raw materials: silk woven in Como, cashmere sourced from Mongolia, and wool selected from the finest merino flocks in New Zealand. These are then shaped by the hands of master craftspeople who have spent decades perfecting their art.
